Somethin' Stupid


Frank Sinatra & Nancy Sinatra

This jazzy pop tune was originally written and recorded by C. Carson Parks in 1966. A year later, Frank Sinatra and his daughter, Nancy Sinatra released a version that became an international hit. This best-known version spent four weeks at number one on Billboard's U.S. Hot 100 Chart, nine weeks on the Easy Listening chart, and eventually became certified gold. Earning Frank his second gold single, and Nancy, her third. Called one of the best duets, this track, with its bolero influence is sure to have anyone swaying side-to-side.

Everything She Wants


Wham!

Wham! was a British pop duo that produced the majorly popular Christmas song, "Last Christmas". Their song Everything She Wants was released on December 3rd in 1984 and was written by George Michael. This disco influenced track includes meaningful lyrics that strayed away from their usual lightweight pop ones, and became the duos third million-selling number-one hit in the United States.
