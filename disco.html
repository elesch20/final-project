Get Down Tonight


K.C. & The Sunshine Band

K.C. & The Sunshine Band was a disco/funk band founded in 1973. Released in 1975, "Get Down Tonight" became their 1st song to hit #1 on the Billboard Hot 100. It displays the fundamental characteristics of a classic disco song, including a fast tempo and catchy lyrics. Notable instruments include an electric guitar recorded at 2x speed giving the song a notable introduction.

You Should be Dancing


Bee Gees

The Bee Gees were a musical trio formed in 1958. Their song "You Should be Dancing"; released in 1976, topped the Billboard Hot 100 for a week. This uptempo disco hit is sure to get anyone dancing, with clean vocals, and polished instrument playing ranging from brass horns to bass guitars, it is one of the most popular disco tracks.
