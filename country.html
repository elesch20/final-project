Mama Tried


Merle Haggard

Mama Tried is a song written by Merle Haggard and his band the Strangers, in 1968. One of Haggard's most famous songs, it won the Grammy Hall of Fame award, and was selected in 2016 as a part of the National Recording Registry for being "culturally, historically, or significant". This song has serious and meaningful lyrics including Haggard's three year incarceration for robbery and all the pain he caused his mother.

Delta Dawn


Tanya Tucker

Tanya Tucker's debut album song Delta Dawn was released in 1972 by Columbia Records when she was only 13 years old. It peaked at number six on the U.S. Billboard Hot Country Singles Chart, and number 72 on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100.
