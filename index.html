You Don't Have to Be a Star (To Be in My Show)


Marilyn McCoo & Billy Davis Jr.

This soul filled 4 minutes and 40 second hit was released in 1976 by the married duo Marilyn McCoo & Billy Davis Jr. This song was included on their I Hope We Get to Love in Time album and had instant success. Selling over one million copies, it spent time as Number 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 and Hot Soul Singles charts, as well as Number 6 on the Easy Listening and Number 7 on the UK Singles Chart. This classic tune eventually certified Gold and won the couple a Grammy.

Being With You



Smokey Robinson

This 1981 title track from Robinson's gold-certified album became his highest charting hit. Spending five weeks at number 1 on the Hot Soul Singles Chart, this easy listening classic is one of Smokey's best songs. This smooth jam is sure to evoke a sense of calm and can make anyone's stress go away.
